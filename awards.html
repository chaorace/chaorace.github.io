Gary Ravenscroft [Teacher]


To Whom It May Concern,


Please accept the following as my recommendation for Christopher L. Crockett.

As his teacher of Economics and Government, I found Christopher to be an exceptional student. He is respectful, diligent, inquisitive, and responsible. He possesses high level academic skills that put him well above his class peers in academic achievement and though he could have easily passed these classes with that much personal effort, Christopher chose to consistently demonstrate a very mature approach to inquiry and problem solving, as well as a highly motivated persistence in meeting the challenges and goals that I established for his class.

In addition to his academic qualities, Christopher works well with others in group activities. His willingness to work cooperatively toward a common goal is a refreshing contrast to the self-indulgence we see too often in his peers. His sense of responsibility is obvious, and his dedication to principle and standards set him apart. He has and shows sincere respect for himself, for his peers, and for his teachers.

It has been my experience with Christopher that he possesses the skills and attitude that make him an exemplary employee, and I sincerely hope that you will consider giving him the opportunity.
