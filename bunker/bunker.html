Bunker Clan Base Directory

Base A

Base A can be accessed from the surface from the bottom of the cave at the largest island at 9500, 22, 20600 Base A exists above the central cave. The central cave is the reference point for accessing all other bases.
Contains:
  • Tree Farm (Oak)
  • Wheat Farm
  • Cow Farm
  • Chicken Farm
  • Common Ores
  • Rare Ores
  • Surface Access
  • Infinite Water Source
  • Crafting Table/Furnace
Base A to Central

Base B

Base B can be found under a small desert island near the largest island at 9570, 36, 20670
Contains:
  • Small Hidden Chest
  • Cactus Plant
  • Surface Access
  • Two water source blocks
  • Crafting Table/Furnace
Central to Base B

Base C

Contains:
