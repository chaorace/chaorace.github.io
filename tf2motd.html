Current Restrictions

  • No sniper rifles -- Huntsman allowed
  • No Loch-n-Load
  • No mini-sentries

Why Restrictions?

Simply put, weapons that can gimp light classes tend to create unfun, unavoidable deaths when wielded by a skilled player. Our goal, that is, to create a fun environment for people to play how they want, is at direct odds with weapons that are uninteractive (sniper rifle, minis) or simply very strong (loch-n-load). The theory of the relationship between interactivity and enjoyment is one that stems heavily from card-games design theory, it's a pretty interesting subject and I adhere to a lot of the ideas it presents.
